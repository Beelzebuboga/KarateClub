SUMMER OUTREACH KARATE PROGRAM

Benefits:

  • Intensive Training: The summer outreach program offers an intensive karate training experience, making it ideal fot those looking to learn quickly or improve their existing skills in a concentrated period.

  • Time-Limited Commitment: If you're unsure about committing to a full membership, the summer program allows you to experience the benefits of karate training without a long-term commitment.

  • Skill Enhancement: For existing members, the summer program can serve as an existing opportunity to enhance your skills and progress faster through the curriculum.

  • Affordable Enrollment Fee: With an enrollment fee of 1000 pesos, the summer program offers a cost-effective way to experience the world of karate.

Why You Should Enroll:

  • The summer outreach program is perfect if you want to get a taste of karate or improve your skills in a relatively short time frame, It offers an intensive training program.

  • If you're considering becoming a member, the summer program can be a great way to test the waters and see if karate is the right fit for you without making a long-term commitment.

  • Existing members can use the summer karate outreach to enhance their skills and progress faster through the curriculum, which is especially beneficial if you're looking to achieve higher belt ranks.

  • If you're looking to make new friends while honing your martial arts skills, the program provides an ideal platform. The camaraderie and support from your peers will enhance your karate experience.
