PROF. PUNDUMA SANI

9th Dan Black Belt - Hanshi

ABOUT OUR FOUNDER

He is the first child of the late Datu Hadji Nur Caris Sumndad and late Baae sa Marawi Hadja Monera Mamalampac Mapandi Sani. He finished his high school at the Dansalan College Foundation, Incorporated. He completely finished the course Bachelor of Science in Commence (Business Administration) at Manuel L. Quezon University, Manila in the year 1968, Academic Requirements leading to M.A.T (PE) at Universoty of the East (Passed COMPRE exam) in 1981. He finished the Academic Requirement M.A.P in Mindanao State University, Marwi City in the year 1997.

ABOUT US

He founded the Mindanao State University Karate Club (MSUKC) on September 15, 1972 and kept his purpose to educate and purely develop the physical, moral, mental and spiritual aspect of the youth. Hanshi Sani believes in helping the youth to be strong, independent, disciplined, and morally stable so they may be consequently fcce their own challenges with the right spirit. He strongly believe that the noble tradition of the MSUKC will never die
